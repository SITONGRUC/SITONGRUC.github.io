/PROJECTS
Automatic Report

As part of my internship at China International Capital Corporation (CICC), I have developed an automated program with the ability to autonomously crawl data, generate automatic visualizations, and create textual content for weekly reports."

Industyr Prediction

As part of my internship at China International Capital Corporation (CICC), I undertook an industry reclassification project for Hong Kong stocks. I leveraged an NLP model trained on A-share stock descriptions, achieving an impressive 90% accuracy for primary industry classification and a commendable 70% accuracy for secondary industry classification, which was pivotal for our rotation strategy.

Styles of Funds of China

As part of my internship at China International Capital Corporation (CICC), I conducted an in-depth analysis of over 3,000 mutual funds. This analysis involved applying the Sharpe and Fama-French three-factor model across various rolling time windows. I rigorously assessed the exposure of these funds to different investment styles, determining both their significance and coefficient values.
